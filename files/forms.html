What To Do After Your Child Has Been Accepted


Congratulations on your child’s acceptance to the Washington University Nursery School! To secure your child’s place as a student, please complete the steps listed below:

Forms Required After Acceptance

  • Enrollment Form: Complete this form every year your child attends WUNS. Enrollment Form
  • Developmental History Form: Complete this after admission to help us understand your child’s developmental background. This only needs to be filled out once. Developmental History Form
  • Medical Forms: Complete the Child Medical Examination Report and Immunization Consent/History forms. Child Medical Examination Report Immunization Consent/History Form

Forms for Returning Students

  • Enrollment Form: Complete this form every year your child attends WUNS. Enrollment Form
